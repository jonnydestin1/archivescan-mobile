Before you scan

ArchiveScan works best when the negative is evenly lit and flat. The app can correct color, but it cannot fully rescue blur, glare, or shadows.

  • Put the negative in front of a bright, evenly lit white screen or light pad.
  • Keep the phone square to the negative.
  • Avoid glare, shadows, and curled film edges.
